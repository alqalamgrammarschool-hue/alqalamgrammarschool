About the School

A campus built on discipline, warmth and ambition

Al-Qalam Grammar School takes its name from the pen — the instrument of knowledge — and treats every lesson as an act of building. Our classrooms combine firm academic structure with genuine care, so students grow into confident, capable and grounded young people.

From the early years through secondary school, teaching at Al-Qalam blends strong fundamentals in language, mathematics and science with attention to character, conduct and community — the qualities that carry students well beyond the classroom.

Frequently Asked Questions

Good to know

What age groups does Al-Qalam Grammar School teach?

We welcome students from the early years through secondary school and matriculation.

How do I apply for admission?

Submit an enquiry through the admissions form above or contact our office directly, and our team will guide you through the next steps.

Does the school offer co-curricular activities?

Yes — sports, clubs, competitions and events run alongside the academic calendar throughout the year.

Can I visit the campus before enrolling?

Absolutely. Campus visits are part of our admissions process — reach out to arrange a convenient time.
